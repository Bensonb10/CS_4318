The report for this phase includes:

* Project title, team name (if there is one), full official names of all team members (5 pts)

* Mission statement. (10 pts)

* Mission objectives. (15 pts)

* Major user views. (15 pts)

* An E/R diagram for your proposed database. Specify key attributes for entity sets, specify relationships with arrowheads. For a team of 2 students, the E/R diagram must have at least 7 entities. For a team of 3 students, the E/R diagram must have at least 9 entities. * A list of uses cases that include actor and step-by-step descriptions.
